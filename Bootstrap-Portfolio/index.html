About Me

Hi! My name is Scott Saloom and welcome to my portfolio. I am from Lafayette, Louisiana, where I received my Bachelor's Degree in Agricultural Economics & Agribusiness from Louisiana State University. I moved to Dallas after graduation in January of 2015.

Currently, I am working an internship as an IT Specialist at Serene Global, which is a residential construction company in Richardson, TX. After completing my 3 year contract with my previous company working in banking compliance industry, I decided it was time for a career change, so that is why I am taking this full stack coding bootcamp at Southern Methodist University.

My excitement level for this class is off the charts. I love computers and working with technology. I believe I will be able to learn enough from this class that I will be able to pursue a new career that will allow me to be successful in the future. I want to be able to tell computers what to do, not them telling me what to do.
